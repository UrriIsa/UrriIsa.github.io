About me

Science & Art

I've focused on the intersection of computer science and biology. However, I enjoy applying the technical foundations of computing in interdisciplinary settings, so I've been working on broadening my perspective.

My Social Service Internship at the Xylarum of the Institute of Biology, UNAM, led to a project that was presented at the XXIII Mexican Botanical Congress, held in Villahermosa, Tabasco. It is currently in the publication process.

Outside of academia, art is one of my passions.
